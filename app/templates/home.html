About Us

The Minds Behind EcoTrack

We are a team of five tech students from Softwarica College of IT and E-commerce who looked at traditional waste management systems and saw an opportunity for innovation. Driven by a shared vision of environmental sustainability and digital transformation, we engineered EcoTrack.

Our platform isn't just about collecting garbage—it's about optimizing routes, tracking live bin data, reducing carbon footprints, and giving municipalities and businesses the data they need to keep our spaces vibrant and clean.
