Our Services

🗑️
Smart Bin Monitoring

IoT-enabled sensors track fill levels in real time and alert teams before bins overflow, reducing complaints and missed pickups.

🛣️
Route Optimization

AI-powered algorithms dynamically plan the shortest, most efficient collection routes, saving fuel and cutting CO₂ emissions.

♻️
Recycling Analytics

Track recycling rates per zone, identify low-compliance areas, and run targeted awareness campaigns backed by real data.

📊
Real-Time Dashboard

A live control centre showing every bin, truck, and collection event across your city on an interactive map.

🚛
Fleet Management

Monitor vehicle health, driver behaviour, and trip logs to keep your waste collection fleet running at peak performance.

📱
Citizen Mobile App

Residents can report illegal dumping, check pickup schedules, and earn eco-points for recycling — all from one easy app.

The Process

How It Works

01
📡
Sense

Smart sensors inside bins measure fill level, weight, and odour 24/7 using ultrasonic and gas technology.

02
☁️
Transmit

Data is sent over LoRaWAN or 4G to EcoTrack's cloud platform in real time, with no manual reporting.

03
🤖
Analyse

Our AI engine processes patterns, predicts fill times, and automatically schedules the optimal collection routes.

04
✅
Act

Drivers receive turn-by-turn route guidance on their phones. Managers review live reports. Cities stay clean.

About Us

The Minds Behind EcoTrack

We are a team of five tech students from Softwarica College of IT and E-commerce who looked at traditional waste management systems and saw an opportunity for innovation. Driven by a shared vision of environmental sustainability and digital transformation, we engineered EcoTrack. Our platform isn't just about collecting garbage—it’s about optimizing routes, tracking live bin data, reducing carbon footprints, and giving municipalities and businesses the data they need to keep our spaces vibrant and clean. At EcoTrack, we believe the future of sustainability lies in smart data.

We're reshaping the future of waste management through innovation and sustainability — bringing cutting-edge IoT and AI solutions that redefine efficiency and environmental consciousness.
